    ChinaZimBuying is a startup company located China owned by a Zimbabwean. It was created to open a road/link between China and Southern African countries. After realizing how cheap and affordable some of the goods are here in China, the founder saw an opportunity to act as bridge assisting those in Africa to buy all kinds of products from through ChinaZimBuying.Here are some of the basics you may need to know about us.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    Here are some of the frequently asked questions by our customers and clients. We hope that this will cover any of the questions that you have.

    • Does the Prices of the products on your pages cover the shipping cost?

      No, all the prices on all of the products on our pages doesn't cover the shipping cost. The shipping cost will be added on top of the product price depending on the weight of the products bought.

    • Which shipping company do you use to ship your clients/customers' goods?

      Some customers/clients have specific shipping companies that they want us to use for shipping their goods. If the clients doesn't specify a shipping company, we can choose the cheapest shipping company available to ship their products.

    • What kind of products can you assist customers to purchase from China?

      All legal products.
